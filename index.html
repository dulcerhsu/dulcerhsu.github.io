Embracing Growth:
The Transformative Power of Therapy


Therapy holds immense importance in our lives, providing a unique space for self-exploration, growth, and healing. It offers a confidential and non-judgmental environment where individuals can express their thoughts, emotions, and experiences freely. Through therapy, we gain a deeper understanding of ourselves, our patterns, and the factors that influence our behaviors. It empowers us to navigate life's challenges, manage stress, and cope with emotional difficulties.

Therapy equips us with valuable tools and strategies to enhance our well-being, build resilience, and cultivate healthier relationships. It promotes self-awareness, self-compassion, and personal growth, allowing us to live more fulfilling and authentic lives. By seeking therapy, we embrace the opportunity to invest in our mental, emotional, and overall holistic well-being.


Areas of Focus

I specialize in providing personalized and empowering therapy services, focusing on holistic well-being and personal growth, tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual. Here are some specific areas where I have extensive experience.

  • Depression

    Together, we can explore the underlying causes of your depression, develop coping strategies tailored to your needs, and work towards restoring a sense of well-being and joy in your life.

  • Trauma

    Through a safe and empathetic therapeutic relationship, we can navigate the impact of your trauma, process painful memories, and cultivate resilience and healing as you reclaim your sense of self.

  • Anxiety

    Let's collaborate on understanding the roots of your anxiety, implementing practical techniques to manage anxious thoughts and sensations, and empowering you to live a more peaceful and fulfilling life.

  • Relationships

    I will support you in improving your relationships by fostering effective communication, promoting understanding and empathy, and equipping you with skills to navigate conflicts and build stronger connections with your loved ones.

  • Stress

    Together, we can identify the sources of stress in your life, explore healthy stress management techniques, and develop personalized strategies to reduce stress levels and enhance your overall well-being.

  • Mindfulness

    Through guidance and practice, I will help you cultivate mindfulness, deepen your present-moment awareness, nurture self-compassion, and integrate mindfulness into your daily life to foster greater peace and clarity.

Hi there, I'm Dulce

I hold a Master's of Science degree in School-Based Family Counseling from California State University, Los Angeles, and a Bachelor's of Arts degree in Psychology with a minor in Spanish from the University of California, Riverside. My educational background, coupled with my hands-on experience, has equipped me with a solid foundation in counseling theories and interventions.

Throughout my career, I have gained valuable experience in providing therapy to diverse populations. As a School Counselor at Ralph Bunche Academy in the Oakland Unified School District, I have worked individually with at-risk students, creating personalized graduation plans, identifying necessary supports and resources, and assisting with postsecondary planning. My time as a Transitional Age Youth Clinician at WestCoast Children's Clinic allowed me to provide trauma-informed psychotherapy and intensive clinical case management to vulnerable children and youth. I also conducted mental health screenings and facilitated access to resources for youth who were sexually exploited or at risk.

In my counseling practice, I prioritize creating a safe and non-judgmental space where clients can explore their emotions, develop coping strategies, and work towards personal growth. I am skilled in conducting comprehensive mental health assessments, formulating treatment plans, and implementing evidence-based therapeutic interventions. With credentials in Pupil Personnel Services, Child Welfare and Attendance, and a License in Marriage and Family Therapy, I bring a strong foundation of knowledge and expertise to my work. Furthermore, my fluency in English and Spanish allows me to effectively communicate and connect with individuals from diverse backgrounds.

I understand that reaching out for therapy can be a courageous step, and I want to assure you that I will provide a confidential and non-judgmental environment where you can freely express yourself. My goal is to establish a therapeutic alliance built on trust, empathy, and mutual respect. Together, we will work towards creating positive change and fostering personal growth.

I am currently accepting new clients and would be delighted to have the opportunity to support you on your journey towards healing and self-discovery. If you are interested in scheduling an initial consultation or have any questions, please do not hesitate to reach out. I am here to listen, support, and guide you every step of the way.

Warm regards,

Dulce Hsu, LMFT
